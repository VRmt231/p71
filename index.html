What's New

  • We have Started online schooling

    In a traditional classroom setting, instructors get more facetime with students, this allows them to gauge students and offer frequent feedback accordingly. The same cannot be said about online classes because the interaction is limited. Online classes have started now and we think this is the best way to ensure academic continuity.

  • You can go back to our school for collecting all your books.

    You can come to our school to collect your ward's books, stationary or anything that they have left behind. Whenever we call you to come to our school, it is for collecting theme-wise booklets that will be made every 3 months.
